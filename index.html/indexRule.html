General Rules & Regulations

  1. English is the medium of instruction in the academy. It is compulsory for all students and teachers to use English language for academic work as well as for conversation in the academy premises.
  2. The behavious of our students, inside and outside the academy is our area and we feel concerned.
  3. A major factor towards success in life is regularity and punctuality. Any work assigned to students should therefore be done in time.
  4. Children coming late to academy without any note from their parents will be penalized.
  5. Any change in the address of the parent/guardian should be informed to the academy office without delay.
  6. Application for leave should be signed by the guardian/parent and must be got sanctioned before availing the leave. absentees are fined Rs. 2.00 per absence.
